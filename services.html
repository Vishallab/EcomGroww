Social media management refers to the process of managing and maintaining an online presence for an individual, business, or organization on various social media platforms. It involves creating, scheduling, publishing, and analyzing content on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, and others, to engage with the target audience, build brand awareness, and achieve marketing objectives.

E-commerce management refers to the overall process of planning, organizing, implementing, and controlling various activities and tasks involved in operating an online business or e-commerce store. It encompasses a wide range of responsibilities, including strategic planning, product sourcing, inventory management, website design and development, online marketing, customer service, order fulfillment, and financial management.

Web development and design are two closely related concepts that involve the creation, optimization, and publishing of websites on the internet. Web deployment refers to the process of making a website accessible to users on the internet, while web design focuses on the visual and functional aspects of a website to create an engaging and user-friendly experience.
